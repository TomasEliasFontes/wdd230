Overview

Site Name

Campana Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide the industry of the Campana area, both as businesses and visitors come to enjoy. The objective is to enhance the labor markets of citizens and attract new inhabitants to it.

Audience

This website is intended for any company or organization that wants to join the chamber. In turn, to promote the same members on this site, sponsor others interested in them. And to the general public who wants to join the institution.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • How can I become a member of the chamber?
  • Who are members of the chamber?
  • What benefits does being on camera offer?
  • How do we attract chamber members?
